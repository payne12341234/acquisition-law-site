Understanding the Acquisition Process

Every deal moves through the same core stages. Knowing what's ahead helps you negotiate from a position of strength.

1

Letter of Intent

Outlining preliminary deal terms, exclusivity, and timeline before formal negotiations begin.

2

Due Diligence

Financial, legal, and operational review to confirm the target's value and surface hidden risk.

3

Deal Structuring

Asset purchase vs. stock purchase, tax treatment, earnouts, and financing considerations.

4

Definitive Agreement

Negotiating representations, warranties, indemnification, and closing conditions.

5

Regulatory Review

Antitrust clearance, industry-specific approvals, and other regulatory filings.

6

Closing & Integration

Final signatures, fund transfers, and post-closing integration planning.
